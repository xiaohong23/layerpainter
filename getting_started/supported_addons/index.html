What does "supported addons" mean?

At the moment there are two addons that are "supported" by BATS. This means that if you have them installed, some of their features are integrated into the BATS workflow in some form.

This does not mean that the code of these addons is in BATS. If you don't have them installed and don't want to, you can disable their information buttons in the settings.

Lily Surface Scrapper

This addon by Ã‰lie Michel allows you to import textures from a URL. This means you can for example go to texturehaven.com and select a textureset. Then copy the URL and enter it in the addons popup.

The addon is supported like this:
When you're editing a Fill Layer, you can press the URL button and enter the URL. BATS will then import the downloaded textures and try to apply them to the correct channel.

This will speed up your workflow a lot, because you don't have to download and select your textures manually, but can just enter the URL and the rest will happen for you.
You can get the addon here.

Data Manager

Disclaimer

This addon is not yet released. As soon as it is, these features will be available.

This addon by Grant Wilk allows you to clean and organize your blend files. You can get statistics about the objects, images, materials and much more in your file.

The addon is supported like this:
There is a small white indicator in the Layer settings panel, which will show you if there are any unused images in your file. When the indicator turns red, you can click it, which will open a popup where you can remove the unused textures.

This will keep your files a lot cleaner when working with a lot of textures.
You can get the addon here.
