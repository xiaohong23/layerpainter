General Behavior

You can add layers to groups by moving them. The layer will get moved into the group when it's directly below or above and is moved in the corresponding direction.

When a layer is in a group it will be affected by the groups mask and visibility. If the visibility of the group is disabled, the layers in the group won't be visible. The mask of the group will be overlayed on the masks of the layer itself.

Groups can't be nested. The button to add a group will be disabled when a layer inside a group is selected or when no layer is in the material.

Moving

When a group is moved, every layer in the group will be moved as well.

When you move a layer out of a group, it won't be affected by the groups settings anymore.

Masks

You can change the mask of the group when you have it selected. It's independent of the channel, but other than that it works the same as the layer masks.
