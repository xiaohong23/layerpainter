What does it do?

A paint layer allows you to paint textures in all the different channels. These textures are transparent by default. When a paint layer is selected in one of the paintable channels (all except the layer channel), Layer Painter will automatically switch to texture painting mode. It will switch out of that mode when a different layer or mask is selected.

What is it for?

You can use a Paint Layer if you want to paint your own textures or want to put stencils on your object. Be aware that Paint Layers will take up the most resources of all layer types.

Settings

Paint Layers only have the general layer settings, as the rest is done with the painted textures.
