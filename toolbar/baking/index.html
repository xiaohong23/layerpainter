Baking Ambient Occlusion

The ambient occlusion baking has the following settings:

  • Resolution: This is the resolution of the baked texture
  • Samples: These are the samples of the ambient occlusion baking

Baking Bevel

The bevel baking has the following settings:

  • Resolution: This is the resolution of the baked texture
  • Samples: These are the samples of the ambient occlusion baking
  • Radius: This is the radius of the bevel, that will be baked
  • Contrast: This is the contrast of the baked texture

Save location

The textures are saved in the folder where your .blend files lives, under _/layerpainter_texture_cache.
