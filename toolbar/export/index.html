Export settings

This will export all your layers to PBR textures. They will get saved in the folder of your .blend file under /pbr_bake.

  • Resolution: This is the resolution of the baked textures
  • Base Color - ORM: Toggles for the channels that you want to be baked
  • Invert green channel: This option will invert the green channel for the baked normap map
  • Overwrite material: This will remove the nodes in your material and setup the textures that you just baked
  • Keep as layers: This will keep the baked textures as a fill layer, so you can continue to edit your material
