Basics

You can find the addon under the materials tab. Here you can switch from the default blender editing to LayerPainter.

Panels

Once you switched to LayerPainter, you'll see multiple panels:

  • Layers Panel: This panel holds the Layer Stack and shows what object you are working on
  • Settings Panel: This panel holds the settings for the selected layer
  • Base Textures Panel: Here you can choose the base texture for your material
  • Baking Panel: Here you can bake textures related to your material
