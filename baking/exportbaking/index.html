Export Baking

Here you can select the channels that you want to bake. They are then in a folder next to your blend file.

You have the option to overwrite the layer stack with the newly baked textures. You can also choose to keep them as a Layer Painter layer.

ORM Baking

Here you can bake a texture with three channels combined to make one texture. You can select what channel should be used for what color channel.
